Professional

Constant Contact Mobile App

Discovery research, brainstorming methods, focus groups, wants and needs analysis, user testing, mobile design

The Project

Ongoing work on the company's mobile app, intended to give customers greater flexibility in accomplishing their goals.

My Role

  • Work in collaboration with staff to conduct rounds of research and iterate on the design of the app.
  • Plan, conduct, and report out on research.
  • Provide design recommendations to product owners and design team members.
  • Provide recommendations for additional research.

Phase I Goal

Identify and obtain access to potential participants for Phase II.

Approach

Attended a meeting of regional development directors -- employees who coordinate and conduct customer training workshops -- in order to:

  1. share information about the role of UX and user research,
  2. establish relationships to foster collaboration on current and future research, and
  3. conduct a pilot test of the brainwriting exercise.

Result

  1. Connections were established to workshop leaders to get access to participants for the initial round of research.
  2. A relationship was initiated with regional development directors.
  3. Validation of the process for the first round of research.

Phase II Goal

Learn more about customers' wants and needs for accomplishing their e-marketing goals when they are away from their primary workspace.

Approach

Conducted brainstorming sessions (using the brainwriting method) with customers and company-affiliated e-marketing consultants to generate ideas.

Result

The initial round of brainwriting resulted in lists of ideas that were used to design Phase III of the research. They also served as supplemental data to use if needed for the focus groups.

Phase III Goal

Continue to generate ideas and determine priorities for those ideas.

Approach

Conducted focus groups with customers to:

  1. repeat the brainwriting exercise to generate additional ideas, and
  2. determine priorities for the generated ideas.

Participants for the focus groups consisted of customers who reported having used the mobile app in the past.

Result

Product owners were presented with a prioritized list of potential functionalities to consider. As a result of the research efforts, the design team focused on making it easier to add and manage contacts using the mobile app.

Phase IV Goal

Assess usability of design iterations.

Approach

Conducted multiple rounds of usability testing on prototype and live versions of the app.

Result

Design recommendations were provided to the design team to inform continued refinement of the app.
