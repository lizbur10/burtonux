Constant Contact Email Editor

Competitive analysis, meta-analysis, user testing, interaction design

The Project

Ongoing research and design work on the company's next-generation email editor.

My Role

  • Work in collaboration with staff to conduct rounds of research and iterate on the design of the editor.
  • Plan, conduct, and report out on research.
  • Provide design recommendations to product owners and design team members.
  • Promote institutional memory of research results and advocate for design decisions that reflect ongoing consistent findings.
  • Provide recommendations for additional research.

Study I Goal

Obtain baseline usability information for the editor against which future improvements will be measured.

Approach

Conducted usability tests of the end-to-end email creation process. Collected qualitative and quantitative information to provide baseline data for each step in the process.

Result

Provided the following to product owners and the design team:

  1. Baseline usability data, including likes/dislikes, pain points, System Usability Scale (SUS) scores, etc.
  2. Design recommendations
  3. Recommendations for subsequent rounds of research.

Study II Goal

Conduct a competitive analysis of competitor products.

Approach

Conducted several online, unmoderated usability tests of competitor products using usertesting.com.

Result

Shared research results, outlining the discoverability, learnability, and usability of the interaction patterns found in the competitors' products.

Study III Goal

Synthesize results from several years' worth of research.

Approach

Reviewed all previous research conducted on the next-generation editor to identify:

  1. the specific tools and interactions being evaluated,
  2. the findings, and
  3. the design recommendations made.

Result

Provided information that helped sustain institutional memory about persistent findings that had not yet been fully addressed and interaction patterns that had been tried in the past.

Ongoing Usability Studies: Goal

Assess usability of design iterations.

Approach

Conducted multiple rounds of usability testing on prototype and live versions of the editor.

Result

Design recommendations were provided to the design team to inform continued refinement of the editor.
